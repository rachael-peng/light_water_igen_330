About the project

In Canada, numerous communities, particularly Indigenous ones, struggle with the persistent challenge of insufficient access to clean water. More than 60 Indigenous communities across Canada have long-standing drinking water advisories (DWAs). These advisories may be due to issues such as water treatment problems, contamination concerns, or outdated infrastructure.

This challenge is especially relevant here in BC, with currently 27 DWAs across 22 First Nations communities in the province.

Insufficient access to clean water extends far beyond the Canadian border, affecting approximately 25% of the world's population. A staggering 771 million people do not have access to clean water close to home.

Our project, Light Water, aims to raise awareness about ongoing challenges related to water drinkability through a dynamic art installation that serves as a focal point for public engagement, education, and advocacy.
